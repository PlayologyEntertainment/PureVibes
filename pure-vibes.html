How to paint
●
Drop — Click or tap anywhere to splatter a paint blob with satellite particles.
✦
Explode — Press and hold, then release. Hold longer for a bigger burst. Watch the charge ring grow.
〜
Drag — Click and drag to fling arcs of paint. Faster drags make denser splatters.
↓
Pour — Select the pour tool, then click and hold to stream paint downward continuously.
⊕
Brush size — Click the size dot in the toolbar to choose Small, Medium, Large, or Huge.
JPG
Save — Click the save icon in the toolbar to download your artwork as a JPG.
